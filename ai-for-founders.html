Who this is for

Built for founders who still carry too much of the operating load themselves.

This workshop is designed for startup founders, founder-operators, small business owners, and agency or consulting founders who are still deeply involved in communication, sales, follow-up, delegation, and execution. No technical background is required.

Communication overload

Too many incoming messages, slow reply cycles, and routine communication that keeps landing back on the founder.

Meetings without movement

Calls happen, but next steps, owners, and deadlines stay fuzzy or disappear completely.

Context trapped in your head

The founder becomes the bottleneck because too much operating context is hard to share, structure, and delegate.

Program

Four weeks. Three working workflows. One cleaner operating rhythm.

The course is outcome-first. Tools matter, but the program is built around recurring founder bottlenecks and practical workflows that can be reused in real work.
Week 0

Setup + Founder Bottleneck Map

Async prep
Participants do
  • Set up Hermes and confirm Claude access
  • Connect Cowork where relevant
  • Map recurring founder bottlenecks
  • Choose 2–3 target workflows for the course
Outcome
  • Working setup
  • Clear bottleneck map
  • Defined focus for the live sessions
Week 1

Communication Workflow

Core workflow
Participants work on
  • Inbox triage
  • Draft replies
  • Follow-up generation

Outcome
  • Working communication workflow

Week 2

Meetings, Decisions, and Delegation

Core workflow
Participants work on

Format & approach

Hands-on, outcome-first, and built on real company materials.

This is not a lecture series and not generic prompt training. Each session is built around practical workflows, short framing, live examples, guided implementation, and outputs participants can use immediately.

Format

4 weeks · 1 live session per week · 2 hours per session · light async preparation between sessions.

Best participation model

Founder + operator / assistant pairs work especially well, but solo founders can join too.

Suggested materials

Bring sample emails, meeting notes or transcripts, proposals, updates, and past founder content where relevant.

Final promise

Move from using AI occasionally to using it as part of how you actually operate.

The goal is not to make participants experts in tools. The goal is to help founders build practical workflows that save time, reduce chaos, improve follow-through, and make them less of a bottleneck in their own company.
