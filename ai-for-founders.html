AI for founders · 4-week workshop

Stop being the bottleneck in your own company.

A practical AI workshop for founders who want better communication, cleaner follow-through, faster delegation, and workflows that stay useful after the course.

Under the hood, the program uses Hermes for operating structure, Claude for reasoning and drafting, and Cowork where connectors and embedded workflows are useful. The course itself is outcome-first.

Who this is for

Built for founders carrying too much of the operating load themselves.

This workshop is designed for startup founders, founder-operators, small business owners, and agency or consulting founders who are still deeply involved in communication, sales, follow-up, delegation, and execution.

Communication overload

Too many incoming messages, slow reply cycles, and routine communication that keeps landing back on the founder.

Meetings without movement

Calls happen, but decisions, next steps, owners, and deadlines stay fuzzy or disappear.

Context trapped in the founder

Too much operating context is hard to share, structure, and delegate. The founder becomes the bottleneck.

What participants leave with

Three practical workflows that can be used immediately in the business.

  • A communication workflow for inbox triage, reply drafting, and follow-up.
  • A meeting-to-action workflow for decisions, owners, deadlines, and delegation briefs.
  • One business-specific growth workflow tailored to the founder’s actual context.
  • A reusable prompt and template library plus a practical 30-day rollout plan.
Program

Four weeks. One clear operating rhythm.

The program is built around recurring founder bottlenecks, not generic tool demos. Each week ends with a working output participants can test on real company material.

Week 0

Setup + founder bottleneck map

Async prep
Participants do
  • Set up Hermes and confirm Claude access.
  • Connect Cowork where relevant.
  • Map recurring founder bottlenecks.
  • Choose 2–3 target workflows for the course.
Outcome
  • Working setup.
  • Clear bottleneck map.
  • Defined focus for the live sessions.
Week 1

Communication workflow

Core workflow
Participants work on
  • Inbox triage.
  • Draft replies.
  • Follow-up generation.
  • Short summaries and handoff notes.
  • Reusable communication templates.
Outcome
  • Working communication workflow.
  • 2–3 reusable templates.
  • One real workflow tested on live company material.
Week 2

Meetings, decisions, and delegation

Core workflow
Participants work on
  • Turning meeting notes or transcripts into summaries.
  • Extracting decisions and unresolved questions.
  • Creating owner and deadline lists.
  • Generating delegation briefs.
  • Producing post-meeting action packs.
Outcome
  • Repeatable meeting-to-action workflow.
  • Reusable delegation template.
  • One real action pack from participant material.
Week 3

Choose your growth workflow

Elective track
Track A

Sales and proposals

  • Proposal drafts.
  • Tailored offers.
  • Follow-up sequences.
  • Reusable sales-writing workflow.
Track B

Founder brand and content

  • Voice and theme capture.
  • Posts from notes or transcripts.
  • Short content plan.
  • Publishable founder-led drafts.
Track C

Investor or stakeholder updates

  • Weekly or monthly updates.
  • KPI narrative framing.
  • Clear asks.
  • Stakeholder memo structure.
Week 4

Capstone + 30-day rollout plan

Operationalization
Participants work on
  • Reviewing all workflows built during the course.
  • Identifying where the founder is still the bottleneck.
  • Deciding what should be daily, weekly, or on-demand.
  • Clarifying founder, operator, and AI roles.
Outcome
  • Three working workflows.
  • Prompt and template library.
  • Practical 30-day rollout plan.
  • Clear path for continued use after the course.
Format & approach

Hands-on. Outcome-first. Real company materials.

This is not a lecture series and not generic prompt training. Each session uses short framing, live examples, guided implementation, and outputs participants can use immediately.

Format

4 weeks. 1 live session per week. 2 hours per session. Light async preparation between sessions.

Best participation model

Founder + operator / assistant pairs work especially well, but solo founders can join too.

Suggested materials

Bring sample emails, meeting notes or transcripts, proposals, updates, and past founder content where relevant.

Final promise

This workshop helps founders move from using AI occasionally to using it as part of how they actually operate.

The goal is not to make participants experts in tools. The goal is to help them build practical workflows that save time, reduce chaos, improve follow-through, and make them less of a bottleneck in their own company.
